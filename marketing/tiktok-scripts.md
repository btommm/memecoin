## General TikTok Tips

- **Post time**: 6–9pm your local time, Tue/Wed/Fri perform best
- **Length**: 30–60 seconds sweet spot for crypto content
- **Hook**: First 3 seconds determine if they scroll — lead with the most interesting claim
- **CTA**: Always end with one clear action ("link in bio", "follow for more")
- **Hashtags**: Mix big (#crypto, #memecoins) with niche (#sui, #shroomcoin, #suinetwork)
- **Trending sounds**: Use trending audio when it fits — algorithm boost
- **Reply to comments**: Respond within the first hour to boost the video
