# SHROOM Telegram Community Setup

## Channel vs Group

Use **both**:
- **@SHROOMcoinANN** — Announcement channel (admin-only posts). For official updates, contract address, exchange listings.
- **@SHROOMcoin** — Community group (everyone can chat). Main community hub.

Link the announcement channel to the group so announcements auto-forward.

## Daily Community Management

**Morning:**
- Check for overnight spam, delete and ban if needed
- Post any news (new exchange, partnership, milestone)

**Afternoon:**
- Engage with chat — reply to genuine questions
- Share Twitter posts in group

**Evening:**
- Celebrate milestones (new holders, price ATH, volume milestones)
- Post memes or fun content
